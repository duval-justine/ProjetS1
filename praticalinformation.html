About Me

I am Justine DUVAL, a first-year student at MMI (Multimedia and Internet Business) in Montbéliard.

I am 18 years old and after having done a general baccalaureate Maths and HGGSP, I came in the MMI GOAL of Montbéliard to become subsequently Infographist. The creation of this site is a project carried out as part of an educational exercise within the institution around the theme of feminism.

So I chose to create a site where people who need to confide can do so through the contact form but also to allow people to learn more about feminism and to have reference figures.
